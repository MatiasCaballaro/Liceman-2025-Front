Liceman is the ultimate platform for managing users, training programs, and leveraging Udemy courses to boost your team's skills.

Key Benefits of Liceman

Streamlined User Management

Easily manage all your users in one centralized platform. Add, update, and remove users with just a few clicks. Control roles and permissions efficiently.

Efficient Training Programs

Create, manage, and track training programs effortlessly. Assign mentors, monitor progress, and get insights into the impact of training on your team.

Seamless Udemy Integration

Integrate with Udemy to unlock a vast library of courses. Assign courses, track learning paths, and keep up with your team's progress on Udemy.

Core Features

  • User Role Management: Define roles like User, Admin, and Mentor.
  • Training Creation: Set up comprehensive training programs.
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor training and course completion.
  • Udemy Course Discovery: Search and assign Udemy courses.
  • Udemy Learning Path: Assign learning paths for your users
  • Udemy Statistics: Keep track of your user statistics.

About Liceman

Liceman was developed to streamline user and training management, leveraging the power of Udemy to provide high-quality learning experiences. It's designed to help organizations enhance their team's skills and knowledge in an organized and efficient manner.
